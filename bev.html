Bev was born in Wellington and was the youngest of six children in her family. Her great-grandfather first came to New Zealand in 1888.

碧紋出生在惠靈頓，是家里六個孩子中最年幼的一個。她的曾祖父於1888年來到新西蘭。

I feel like a stranger living in New Zealand because I have no family here … I still feel like a Chinese Chinese … I haven’t got used to living in the Kiwi way.

因為在新西蘭沒有家，我覺得自己在這裡是一個陌生人 …… 我仍然感覺自己是更純粹的華人 …… 我還沒有習慣像新西蘭人一樣生活。
