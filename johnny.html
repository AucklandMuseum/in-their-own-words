Listen to Johnny's story

Johnny Hui (Hui Chung Hang)

許中行

Family and early years programmer

家庭暨幼年觀眾活動項目經理

Johnny was born in Hong Kong and moved to New Zealand, aged four, with his family in 1989. He is equally proud of both his Chinese and New Zealand heritage.

許中行出生在香港，1989年，四歲時，隨家人來到新西蘭。他對自己所承襲的中國及新西蘭文化遺產懷有同等自豪感。

I would classify myself as a New Zealander Chinese … It’s a difficult and interesting concept – is it New Zealand Chinese or Chinese New Zealander?

我願意將自己歸類為新西蘭華人 …… 這是一個難以理解、有意思的概念。是新西蘭的華人還是華裔新西蘭人呢？
