Cara comes from Yantai, Shandong province, in northern China and has been studying in New Zealand for one and a half years. She’s undecided if she’ll stay and has a dream to travel the world.

王思琦，來自中國北方的山東省煙台市，已經在新西蘭留學一年半時間。她有環遊世界的夢想，還沒有決定是否將留在新西蘭。

I feel like a stranger living in New Zealand because I have no family here … I still feel like a Chinese Chinese … I haven’t got used to living in the Kiwi way.

因為在新西蘭沒有家，我覺得自己在這裡是一個陌生人 …… 我仍然感覺自己是更純粹的華人 …… 我還沒有習慣像新西蘭人一樣生活。
