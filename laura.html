Laura Huang (Huang Wan-Hsun)

黃琬勛

Senior retail lead

零售業務高級經理

Laura was 15 when she came to New Zealand from Taipei, Taiwan, over 23 years ago. Initially her family remained in Taiwan, so she lived with a Kiwi family.

23年前， 15歲的黃琬勛從台北來到新西蘭。最初，她的家人依然生活在台灣，所以她寄住在一個新西蘭人家庭里。

I was born in Taiwan but I grew up in New Zealand … I could call myself a Kiwi, a Chinese Kiwi.

我出生在台灣，但是成長在新西蘭 …… 我稱自己是一個新西蘭人，一個華裔新西蘭人。
